Since the early 1990s, Tristar Producer Services has focused on growth in Texas, Oklahoma, New Mexico, Louisiana, and Colorado. Tristar has a proven track record managing gas production from the wellhead to market for over 25 years.

Allow us to handle all your gas marketing needs, so that you can be freed up to focus on your exploration and production activities.

As a producer of natural gas, your goal is to move your product downstream at a competitive price. You need knowledgeable professionals, along with flexible terms and service options. Our experienced staff is focused on customer service and a variety of pricing options, comprehensive distribution capabilities, and timely and accurate payments. We provide revenue distribution for working interest owners and help the producer with a variety of services, including gas balancing, severance tax remittance and reporting. Outsourcing these time-intensive functions will increase your productivity, and decrease the costly errors of inaccurate reporting.
